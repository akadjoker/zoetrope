Sound

A sound is an instance of a source audio file, e.g. an mp3.  A sound must be added somewhere either in your app’s view or meta view in order for it to work properly.  A sound that is not added to a view will still play, but changing its properties will not work.

source

The audio source to use.  See https://love2d.org/wiki/Source for details.  This cannot be shared with any other Sound object.
